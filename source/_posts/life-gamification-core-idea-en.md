# Life Gamification - Core Idea

Treat life as a game, making it more interesting and meaningful by setting goals, earning experience points, and unlocking achievements.

## Main Concepts

1. **Quest System**: Gamify daily tasks
2. **Achievement System**: Record important milestones
3. **Growth System**: Track personal growth trajectory
